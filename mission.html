Background

At Chisuma Foundation, we are driven by a single goal; to do our part in making the world a better place for all. Our decision making process is informed by comprehensive empirical studies and high quality data evaluation. We strive to build productive relationships and make a positive impact with all of our pursuits. ​​

Facilitate or promote sustainable development programmes in the area through internet interaction and any other legal ways of communication.

To preserve, develop and help protect the art and culture of the area through the promotion of art and cultural activities in the area also through cultural exchange programmes with other international communities.

To help and promote environmental awareness programmes through interacting with authorized national and international environmental groups.

To help, support and promote youth educational and sporting activities.

Water & Sanitation
