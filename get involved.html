Volunteering at Chisuma Foundation

We have several programs that run throughout the year. Individual and group volunteers are always needed to assist with making the work of this Foundation a sucees. You can get involved by volunteering or donating a special skill to any existing or new project. Join our group of volunteers who make themselves available year-round as needed.

Costs

Our program requires that the volunteer fully cover all costs associated with his/her assignment.

How to Apply

To volunteer, please email your contact information to info@chisumafoundationtrust.org If you do not want to volunteer, you may choose to donate towards any project of your choice.
